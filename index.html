Frequently Asked Questions

How long does integration take?

Our typical integration takes less than 5 minutes with our comprehensive SDKs and documentation.

Which stablecoins do you support?

We only support stablecoins (USDT, USDC and EURC) in Solana or Base chain.

Do customers need to connect their wallets?

No! This is one of our key advantages. Customers can make payments without connecting their wallets, eliminating security concerns and providing a much smoother user experience.

How do I receive payment notifications?

We support webhooks for real-time payment notifications to your server. You can also use Discord webhooks for instant notifications. All payment events are also tracked in your dashboard.

What happens if a payment expires?

Payment sessions automatically expire after 10 minutes for security reasons. If a payment expires, you'll receive a notification via webhook (if configured) and can create a new payment link or session as needed.

How quickly are payments processed?

Payments are processed instantly on Solana and Base networks. You'll receive real-time notifications when payments are completed, and funds are available immediately in your custodial balance.

Can I test the integration before going live?

Absolutely! You can try our demo to see how the payment flow works, and we provide comprehensive documentation with examples to help you test your integration before launching.
