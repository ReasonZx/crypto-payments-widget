Core Concepts

Before diving into specific integrations, it's helpful to understand some key concepts:

Vendor ID

Your unique identifier that connects payments to your account. Find this in your dashboard settings.

Supported Chains

Stablio supports multiple blockchains including Solana and Base to ensure fast and cheap transactions.

Custodial Wallet

Stablio can manage wallets for you or forward payments directly to your own wallets.
